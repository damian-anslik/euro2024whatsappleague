  1. Predictions must be submitted before the match starts. Users can update their predictions at any time up until the match begins.
  2. Points are awarded as follows:
    • Correct score: 5 points
    • Correct margin (difference between the goals): 3 points
    • Correct outcome (win, lose, or draw): 1 point
    • Incorrect prediction: 0 points
  3. Only the highest applicable points will be awarded for each prediction. For example, if a user predicts a score of 2-1 and the match ends 2-1, they will receive points for the correct score only (5 points).
  4. Points are based on the score at the end of regular time. Goals scored in extra time do not count.
  5. Scores are updated every 5 minutes.
  6. If there is a tie in the total points, the user with the most correct score predictions will be ranked higher.
